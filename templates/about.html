My Story

Crafting Visual Masterpieces

With over 5 years behind the lens, I've had the incredible privilege of documenting life's most meaningful moments — from the nervous excitement of wedding mornings to the pure, unscripted joy of family gatherings.

My approach is simple: I believe every person has a unique story, and my role is to tell it authentically. I combine technical mastery with a genuine human connection to ensure you feel completely comfortable and natural in front of the camera.

Based in New York, I travel worldwide for the right story. Whether it's an intimate elopement in the mountains or a grand ballroom wedding, I bring the same level of dedication, creativity, and passion to every single shoot.
